Deoxy AI – Making Learning Easier for Dyslexic Students
Deoxy AI is an innovative platform designed to make education accessible, interactive, and personalized for students with dyslexia. By combining AI and IoT technologies, it transforms conventional learning into a supportive and engaging experience.

Why Deoxy AI?

Millions of students face challenges with reading, writing, and focus due to dyslexia.
Traditional tools often overlook accessibility, leading to frustration and disengagement.
Deoxy AI bridges this gap with smart, inclusive solutions tailored to each learner.

Key Features

Eye-Tracking Brightness Control → Adjusts lighting for comfortable reading.
Text-to-Speech & Speech-to-Text → Makes content easier to read and write.
Visual Learning with AI → Converts text into images for better understanding.
IoT Focus Mode → Minimizes distractions using smart devices.
Multilingual Support → Real-time translation for diverse learners.
Gamified Learning → Tracks progress and motivates students with rewards.

Why It Stands Out

Designed for Accessibility → Fonts, colors, and layouts optimized for dyslexia.

AI + IoT Integration → Goes beyond standard edtech platforms.

Inclusive & Engaging → Supports multiple languages and interactive learning.

Impact-Driven → Enhances learning outcomes and makes education enjoyable.
